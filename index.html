Iphone 15

The iPhone 15, launched in late 2023, is a popular smartphone known for its Dynamic Island, powerful A16 Bionic chip, and 48MP Main Camera, bringing features from the Pro line to the standard model, with USB-C connectivity and a bright 6.1-inch display.
